Welcome to the Blog About Itself

This isn’t just another demo site. The Minimal Apple-Style Blog is both a free blog template and a living guide that documents how it works. Every post you see here is written in the same simple format that you can use for your own blog — plain HTML files with clean design and ready-to-use ad slots.

Why a Self-Documenting Blog?

Most templates come with a boring README file. This project takes a different path: the documentation is the blog itself. Each article teaches you how to set up, customize, and monetize your own version. In other words, you’re reading the instructions while seeing them in action.

Easy Setup, Zero Hassle

  • 🚀 No database, no CMS, no subscriptions.
  • 📝 Just drop your .html posts into the articles/ folder.
  • 🌍 Works instantly on GitHub Pages, Netlify, or any static host.
  • 💸 Built-in ad slots for monetization — no extra setup required.

Passive Income Potential

Let’s be real: not every blog makes money. But this template removes all the excuses. You don’t need to pay for hosting, subscribe to a service, or struggle with technical setup. All you need is your content and a free hosting account. Ads can handle the rest — giving you a chance at passive income with almost zero investment.
